Craftsmanship

Our craft


Dedicated craftsmen hailing from various nations and lifestyles, we united under one single pretense: Creation.
For this is what ties us craftsmen together and pushes us to go beyond our artistical limit and bring our craft to another level. One where each and every piece will be thorougly planned and one of a kind. In our pursuit of perfection, we will only create unique pieces of craftsmanship.

Jewelry

Our Jewels


Our craftsmen deliver pieces of jewelry on an average basis of up to 10 new and unique products per week. These jewels range from a simple tiara to very refined signet rings. Those artworks and the gender targeted are both decided by the artist which will, as always, leave a pamphlet concerning all the detail of this unique jewel.

Unique weapons

Our Weapons of Collection


The forges burn endlessly in our pursuit of perfection. However, only 3 unique weapons of collections will make it out each week. For such works require a high amount of time, effort, and planning ; the likes of which you will be able to buy directly from its craftman if you so desire.

Watches

Our Watches


No amount of words will suffice to describe the calm and meticulousness of out watchcrafters. With years of experience under their belt, they do not limit themselves to anything and keep trying to reach new heights in their art. Up to 4 new watches will get released every week. Custom orders have more chances to get validated by them than by our other crafters. As such, do not hesitate to use the contact form below to reach out to us.
